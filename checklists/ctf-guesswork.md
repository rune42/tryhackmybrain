### Iterative loop
1. Identify the one most relevant lead you find after the first enumeration.
2. Attempt to follow it with an appropriate method. If it fails, probe with another method.
3. After 2-3 failed probes, park the lead and switch to the second most relevant one.
4. If two consecutive probes take more than 20 minutes of active testing, find the most brute-forceable lead and run an automated probe. Repeat.
